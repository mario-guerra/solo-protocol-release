### üìã PRD Standard Template

Every PRD **must** follow this high-fidelity structure:

1. **Executive Summary:** Vision and Core Value Proposition.
2. **Problem Statement:** Specific pain point and urgency.
3. **User Personas:** Goals, pain points, and behaviors.
4. **User Journeys:** Step-by-step flow with friction point mapping.
5. **Feature Requirements:** Prioritized list (P0, P1, P2) with descriptions and binary acceptance criteria.
